### 🎯 Unit Goals


Welcome to Unit 7! This unit will deepen your understanding of recursion by teaching you to write recursive functions in Python, focusing on identifying base cases and recursive calls. Additionally, you will explore key search algorithms, most notably binary search, understanding their applications and efficiencies.


By the end of this unit, you will be able to:


- Understand the difference between iterative and recursive problem solving approaches

- Implement solutions using a recursive technique

- Describe and apply a divide and conquer approach to problems

- Solve problems using binary search
