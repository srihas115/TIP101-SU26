### 🎯 Unit Goals


Welcome to Unit 4! You will learn a technique called the two pointer method. A pivotal strategy in algorithmic problem solving, particularly beneficial for optimizing operations on lists and strings. This unit covers a spectrum of applications from data manipulation tasks, such as reversing lists, removing duplicates, and more. The systematic exploration of this technique underpins much of the unit's focus, guiding you through its application in both numerical computations and data structure optimizations.


By the end of this unit, you will be able to:


- Solve intermediate list, string, and dictionaries problems

- Apply the two-pointer method to a variety of list/string problems

- Use an enhanced understanding of data traversal dynamics to solve problems related to ordered sequences with greater algorithmic efficiency

- Solve complex problems involving multiple conditions, such as finding pairs with a given sum or merging intervals
