### 🎯 Unit Goals


In this final unit, the problem sets and assessment will feature questions spanning topics from Units 1-9. Like you will experience in a real interview, questions may be those you've already encountered earlier in your studies, or they may be brand new to you! You'll use the skills you strengthened in preceding units to match problems to the proper technique and/or data structure without any hints.


Additionally, the [cheatsheet](https://courses.codepath.org/courses/tip101/unit/10#!cheatsheet) for this unit will introduce helpful concepts like list comprehensions, ternary operators, and sets that will aid you in writing more optimal, concise code.


By the end of this unit, you will be able to:


- Choose an appropriate algorithmic technique and data structure to solve problems covering Unit 1-9 content

- Use ternary operators to write conditional statements in a single line

- Use list and dictionary comprehensions to create lists and dictionaries concisely
