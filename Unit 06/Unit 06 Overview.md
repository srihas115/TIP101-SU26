### 🎯 Unit Goals


Welcome to Unit 6! In this unit, you will continue to build your skills traversing and manipulating linked lists. You will learn to solve problems involving linked lists variations including circularly linked lists, doubly linked lists, and linked lists with cycles. This unit will also cover advanced algorithmic techniques for linked lists including creating a temporary head node, performing multiple passes of a linked list, and detecting cycles in a linked list using a variation of the two-pointer technique.


By the end of this unit, you will be able to:


- Solve problems involving singly, doubly, and circularly linked lists

- Use the slow and fast pointer method to detect cycles in a linked list

- Create a temporary head node to solve problems that require manipulating a linked list's head node

- Solve problems requiring multiple traversals of a linked list

- Evaluate the time and space complexity of problems involving linked lists
